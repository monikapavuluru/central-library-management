Welcome to VEMU Central Library

About The Department Library System is a web-based application developed as part of our Full Stack Development project.
It is designed to digitalize and automate the traditional library process.
This system:
Maintains book records
Manages student and staff details
Tracks issued and returned books
Reduces manual work
Our goal is to make the library more efficient, organized, and easily accessible.
